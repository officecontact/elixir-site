How we calculate this

Traditional cost = team size × hours × hourly rate ÷ team size, i.e. straight hours × rate. The team-size slider scales the timeline (more engineers → shorter calendar weeks, same total hours).

Elixir cost assumes vibe coding compresses required hours by the speed multiplier (default 5×) and reduces team count to a small senior pod (≤3) — both effects compound. Default assumptions are conservative; on most engagements the gap is wider.
